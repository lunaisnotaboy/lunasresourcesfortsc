About This Website

If you're wondering, "Who made this wonderful website?!", you're either a student who needs to finish something before a deadline, or you're a teacher who needs to recycle old work.

Either way, welcome! My name is Luna Nova, and I am a student at Tippecanoe School Corporation , and I have gotten a little frustrated with the way TSC has handled assignments the past few years.

After all the assignments had gotten spread out over Canvas, MathXL, and many other services, I found myself struggling to keep up with everything.

So that's why I created this site. It aims to provide resources, (but not answers) for homework and projects given to students by various teachers.

I genuinely hope that all of this will be useful for many people.

~ Luna
